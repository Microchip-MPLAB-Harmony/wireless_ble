Transparent Credit Based Server Role Requirements

The Transparent Credit Based Server shall instantiate one Transparent Credit Based Service [1]. A higher layer specification may impose additional requirements on the service declaration of the Transparent Credit Based Service (e.g., «Primary Service») as well as other additional requirements. There shall not be more than one instance of the Transparent Credit Based Service that is declared as a «Primary Service».
